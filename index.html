About me

Hey! I'm a Web and UI/UX designer with a creative background in floral design — I spent 8 years working with flowers, colours, and layouts, which naturally led me into the world of digital design. Now, I love creating clean, user-friendly websites using tools like Figma, Adobe, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript.

I’ve got a degree in Nanotechnology and Automation Engineering and have always been into tech — that’s why I’m currently studying Full Stack Development at Keyin College, learning how to bring both the design and functionality of websites to life. I’m also into data analysis — I enjoy working with Python, love a good math challenge, and I’m always curious about what data can tell us.

When I’m not designing or coding, I’m probably planning a trip or daydreaming about one. I worked as a travel manager for 2 years, and I’ve been hooked on travel and the hospitality world ever since. Exploring new places and meeting people from different cultures inspires a lot of what I do creatively.
